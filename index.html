FAQsPopular QuestionCommon inquiries from organizations looking to harness digital transformation for development across Africa.

Do I need a digital strategy to work with you?

Not necessarily. We meet you where you are. Whether you have a complete digital strategy or just a vision, our team can assess your needs and co-develop a roadmap tailored to your development goals and operational context.

How do your tech solutions support humanitarian or development work?

We design systems and digital tools that simplify data collection, increase transparency, and scale outreach. Whether it’s health, education, or agriculture—we create smart, scalable solutions for real-world problems in African contexts.

Can Enazen help us digitize our existing programs?

Yes. Whether you're moving from paper-based processes or looking to modernize outdated systems, we conduct audits, propose tools, and develop customized platforms to digitize workflows efficiently and securely.

How long does it take to implement a digital solution?

It depends on the scope, but typical project timelines range from 4 to 12 weeks. We work in agile sprints and always align delivery to your urgency and capacity, ensuring rapid, meaningful rollout.

Do you offer training for local teams after deployment?

Absolutely. Capacity building is central to our approach. We provide hands-on training, user manuals, and ongoing support so your team can confidently manage and scale the solution long after launch.

What types of organizations do you work with?

We partner with NGOs, government agencies, donor-funded programs, private companies, and social enterprises across Africa—particularly those focused on impact-driven initiatives like health, education, and agriculture.
